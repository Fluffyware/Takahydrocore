Marine Geophysical & Vibrocore

Geophysical & Vibrocore Survey
Karimun Besar Island

Riau Islands, Indonesia · 2025

Scope of Work

  • Equipment & Supply

    Provision and maintenance of tools, equipment, and supplies for Sub-Bottom Profiler and Vibrocore campaigns throughout the survey duration.

  • Vessel Provision

    Supply of survey vessel capable of supporting both the geophysical and vibrocore campaign operations simultaneously.

  • Mobilization & Demobilization

    Full mobilization and demobilization of site survey and soil investigation equipment to and from the survey location.

  • Sub-Bottom Profiler / Geophysical Survey

    Geophysical survey covering approximately 225 km of survey lines, encompassing Single Beam Echo Sounder (SBES), Multi-Beam Echo Sounder (MBES), Side Scan Sonar (SSS), and Sub-Bottom Profiler (SBP).

  • Vibrocore Campaign

    Execution of 50 vibrocore points (minimum 40 successful points). All core samples securely packed and preserved. KUM operator stationed on board throughout operations. Laboratory testing performed on all recovered samples.

  • Reporting

    Submission of a comprehensive Factual Report covering all survey and vibrocore results, data interpretation, and laboratory findings.
