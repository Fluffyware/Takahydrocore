Departments

Find where your work fits inside THI.

THI project delivery depends on field crews, technical teams, project control, business support, and people operations working in the same rhythm.

01 / Department Directory

Department areas

Each department supports a different part of the project chain.

01 / Field execution

Geotechnical Operations

Handles soil investigation work across offshore, nearshore, and onshore scopes, including drilling, CPT, sampling, coring, and field crew coordination.

DrillingCPTSamplingSite crews
Typical focus

Preparing methods, operating field equipment, recording site progress, and keeping the investigation workflow controlled.

Good background

Civil engineering, geotechnical engineering, geology, earth science, or hands-on field operation exposure.

02 / Survey and data

Geophysical Operations

Supports marine and land geophysical survey work, from bathymetry and positioning to seismic acquisition, onboard monitoring, and survey data workflow.

BathymetryPositioningSeismicData acquisition
Typical focus

Operating survey instruments, checking acquisition quality, monitoring data, and coordinating with field teams.

Good background

Geophysics, hydrography, oceanography, marine science, surveying, or data processing interest.

03 / Technical delivery

Engineering

Turns project requirements and field data into practical methods, engineering review, interpretation, reporting, and deliverable control.

MethodReviewReportingInterpretation
Typical focus

Preparing method statements, reviewing data, supporting technical decisions, and checking report quality.

Good background

Civil, geotechnical, ocean, geological, or survey engineering with strong technical writing habits.

04 / Compliance and readiness

QHSE

Maintains quality, health, safety, and environmental control through inspections, risk assessment, documentation, induction, and HSE programs.

Risk controlInspectionInductionAudit support
Typical focus

Supporting permits, emergency readiness, toolbox routines, compliance records, and worksite discipline.

Good background

Occupational safety, environmental engineering, compliance, quality systems, or field HSE exposure.

05 / Asset readiness

Facility & Equipment Support

Keeps equipment, workshop preparation, spare parts, and field assets ready so project teams can mobilize with reliable support.

WorkshopMaintenanceSpare partsMobilization
Typical focus

Equipment checks, maintenance planning, asset tracking, logistics support, and readiness before field execution.

Good background

Mechanical, electrical, industrial engineering, workshop support, maintenance, logistics, or asset control.

06 / Project and client control

Project Control & Commercial

Connects client requirements with execution through tender support, project administration, schedule tracking, cost control, and contract coordination.

TenderCost controlScheduleContract
Typical focus

Tracking project progress, preparing commercial documents, organizing client requirements, and supporting contract administration.

Good background

Project management, industrial engineering, business administration, finance, contract, or tender administration.

07 / Supply and purchasing

Procurement

Supports project and office needs through vendor coordination, purchasing administration, quotation comparison, material follow-up, and delivery readiness.

PurchasingVendorQuotationDelivery
Typical focus

Coordinating purchase requests, checking supplier documents, following up materials, and keeping procurement records traceable.

Good background

Procurement, supply chain, business administration, logistics, vendor management, or purchasing support.

08 / Market visibility

Marketing & Client Relations

Builds THI visibility through company profiles, client communication, proposal support, project references, and business presentation material.

Client relationProposalProfilePresentation
Typical focus

Preparing reference materials, keeping communication clear, supporting proposals, and presenting THI capability professionally.

Good background

Marketing, communication, business, proposal writing, client relation, visual documentation, or presentation support.

09 / Financial discipline

Finance & Accounting

Manages financial control, accounting records, project cost administration, invoicing support, payment tracking, and financial reporting.

AccountingCost adminInvoiceReporting

Maintaining financial records, supporting project budgets, checking payments, and keeping reporting clean and traceable.

Accounting, finance, tax, project finance administration, financial reporting, or data discipline.

Human Resources

Supports recruitment, employee administration, personnel development, training coordination, and people-related needs across office and field teams.

RecruitmentTrainingPeople dataDevelopment

Human resources, psychology, recruitment, employee administration, training coordination, or HR operations.
