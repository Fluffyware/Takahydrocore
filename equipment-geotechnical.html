Laboratory

08 / Soil testing and documentation

Soil & Geotechnical Laboratory

The soil and geotechnical laboratory capability is presented in collaboration with PT Hydrocore. It supports sample handling, basic and advanced soil testing, rock testing, controlled storage, and ISO/IEC 17025 accredited laboratory workflow.

Basic Lab Test

  • Soil classification: moisture content, Atterberg limits, sieve and hydrometer, linear shrinkage, specific gravity, and organic content.
  • Soil chemical and corrosivity: pH, electrical resistivity, carbonate, sulfate, chloride, and salinity on extracted pore water.
  • Oedometer, permeability, mini lab vane, unconfined compression, UU/CD direct shear, UU triaxial, CiU/CiD triaxial, and rock strength testing.

Advanced Lab Test

  • X-Ray CT Scan, CRS consolidation, thixotropy, static simple shear, and cyclic simple shear.
  • Bender element and resonant column testing for advanced soil behavior assessment.
  • Humidity and temperature controlled storage room with capacity for 300 samples.

The most advanced and complete soil laboratory, ISO/IEC 17025 accredited.

Integrated geophysical, geotechnical, drilling, laboratory, and field support capability for marine and land projects.
